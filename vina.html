About Us

Vhina's Treat is a premier destination for authentic Nigerian cuisine and exquisite catering services in Lagos. We specialize in flavorful jollof rice, succulent small chops, creamy puff puff, and custom party platters that delight every palate. Perfect for birthdays, corporate events, weddings, and family gatherings, our fresh ingredients and traditional recipes ensure unforgettable flavors. Discover the true taste of Nigeria with us!

From intimate dinners to grand celebrations, Vhina's Treat handles it all with passion and precision. Our menu features rich Okra soup, aromatic rice with chicken and plantain, and indulgent bomboloni desserts. Committed to quality, hygiene, and customer satisfaction, we deliver joy through authentic Nigerian flavors. Book your event today!
